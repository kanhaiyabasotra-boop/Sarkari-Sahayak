Hamare Baare Mein

Sarkari Sahayak ek swatantra jaankari website hai — hum kisi sarkari vibhaag ka hissa nahi hain. Hamara maqsad seedha hai: Aadhar, PAN, Ration Card, Voter ID jaise rozmarra ke sarkari kaam ko, unhi shabdon mein samjhana jo aam log samajh sakein.

Har guide official portals ke current process ko dekh kar likhi jaati hai, aur samay-samay par update ki jaati hai taaki jaankari purani na ho jaaye.

Agar aapko koi guide mein galti mile ya koi process change ho gaya ho, toh humein contact page ke through zaroor bataayein.
